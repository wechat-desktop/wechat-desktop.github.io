System requirements

Windows 11 or Windows 10 (64-bit recommended). Admin rights may be required for installation.

How to install

  1. Click “Download for Windows (64-bit)”.
  2. Open the installer and follow on-screen steps.
  3. Launch WeChat and sign in on your PC.

How to uninstall

  1. Open Settings → Apps → Installed apps.
  2. Find WeChat → Uninstall → Confirm.
  3. Restart if prompted.
